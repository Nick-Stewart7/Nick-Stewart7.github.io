Cookup!

What is it?

Cookup is a recipe sharing platform designed to bring people together over common tastes and dietary patterns. We made Cookup in order to give individuals with bangin' recipes a place to share them. As well as, giving a space for people to share recipes specific to their dietary restrictions. Cookup has a forum type style and once a user signs in a user can fully customize their feed, post and save recipes.

What I learned

I learned what it really means to completely develop an app from idea to deployment. I learned frontend design principles like wireframing and mocking. I learned how to implement 2-Factor-Authentication with Twilio. I got more experience with database design and management. I Affirmed my knowledge of ExpressJS, functional programming using React and automatic deployments with Heroku. Lastly I reinforced what I have learned about using Git version control, development in AWS and AGILE delivery.
